How To Play?

There are many sections on the webpage, each one is a task.
Get a clue by solving a puzzle at top of website, to know which task you have to solve out of all tasks. Repeat this after every checkpoint.
You get checkpoint after completing a task.
There can be some secret tasks(easter eggs). If you find any and solve them, you get extra credits.
There can be multiple solutions to some tasks, you have to answer the relevent one. Otherwise you can get stuck further in game!
You can see how many checkpoints you have collected on the map at top of webpage of game.
